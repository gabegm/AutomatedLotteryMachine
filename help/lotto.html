Buying a lotto ticket

Once you press C to continue, you shall be greeted with the screen below.

To buy a lotto ticket, press L to continue.

Once you input the amount of money you wish to place, press enter.

Next you will have three choices to make from being Prima, Ambo, or Terno.

Now you have the choice of either generating random numbers, or inputting the numbers yourself, press Y/N accordingly.

If you choose yes, you shall be shown with this message.

However if you choose no, you shall be shown with this message instead.

How to win

Prima

You must choose a number between 1 and 90, and you only win if your number is drawn first out of the 5 numbers from the lottery draw. (60 times stake)

Ambo

You must choose 5 numbers between 1 and 90, and you only win if you guess 2 numbers out of the 5 numbers from the lottery draw. (215 times stake)

Terno

You must choose 5 numbers between 1 and 90, and you only win if you guess 3 numbers out of the 5 numbers from the lottery draw. (3500 times stake)
