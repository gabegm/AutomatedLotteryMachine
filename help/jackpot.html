Buying a lotto ticket

Once you press C to continue, you shall be greeted with the screen below.

To buy a jackpot ticket, press J to continue.

Now you have the choice of either generating random numbers, or inputting the numbers yourself, press Y/N accordingly.

If you choose yes, you shall be shown with this message.

However if you choose no, you shall be shown with this message instead.

How to win

When there's a winner

In order to win you must guess all 5 numbers, and if you do you win €200,000.

When there's no winner

If no one wins, the jackpot is increased by €100,000 for the next draw.
